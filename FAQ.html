FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are your restaurant timings??
We are open from 10 AM to 9 PM on all weekdays.

Does your restaurant provide special arrangement for kid?
Yes, we have a kids play area for children for age group 4 to 10 years.

Does your restaurant have separate kitchen for veg and non-veg?
Yes, we use separate kitchen and utensils for veg and non-veg food. We even have different chefs for both.

I booked a table but something came up. Can I reschedule?
Whether you’re having technical difficulties or something comes, please email us via our Help Desk with your confirmation number and we’ll help you reschedule for a future date.

Does your restaurant provides delivery?
We will be available soon for delivery on Zomato and Swiggy.

Can your restaurant provide dishes according to the customer’s taste or you stick to your own recipies only?
Our trained chef make food with our best recipes and with all our love. We do follow our own recipes according to our restaurant policy but you can ask for changes in the spices according to your tastebuds beforehand while making an order.

Your restaurant have waiter service or self service?
Our restaurant have self service policy but we provide waitors to help our customers if they book for a table on their special occasions.

Can I dine-in without any prior booking?
We will provide you an unreserved table if available or you have to wait for your chance. We encourage to pre-book your table to avoid any unconvinience.

Can I get my refund back on cancelling a booking?
Your refund will be processed within 24 hour of your cancellation. We encourage you to cancel the booking atleast 12 hours before so we can arrange for our other customers in waiting.
